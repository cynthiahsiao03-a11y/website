About Me

Integrated Engineering Student at UBC (Mechanical & Civil)

Cynthia Hsiao

Burnaby, BC, Canada

I am an Integrated Engineering student at the University of British Columbia specializing in mechanical and civil engineering. I am passionate about designing and building real-world systems through hands-on prototyping, structural design, and manufacturing.

My experience includes composite fabrication, mechanical analysis, and collaborative engineering projects. I have contributed to structural design and manufacturing through engineering design teams, focusing on creating sustainable, practical, efficient, and reliable solutions.

Design Teams and Clubs

Involvement in UBC Engineering Teams and Leadership Roles

UBC Submarine Design Team (SUBC) – Hull and Frame Team Member (Jan 2026 – Present)
Designed and optimized structural connectors to reduce hull deformation and improve load transfer within a human-powered submarine system. Manufactured composite hull components using multi-layer fiberglass layups with controlled fiber orientation, and collaborated across sub-teams to ensure alignment between structural design, manufacturability, and assembly constraints.

UBC Women in Engineering (WiE) – Co-Vice President, WiSE Committee (Sep 2024 – Present)
Led cross-functional teams to plan and deliver large-scale events, securing $13,000+ in sponsorship funding. Managed budget allocation and facilitated structured meetings to ensure efficient execution and strong industry and student engagement.

UBC Concrete Toboggan – Electronics Design Team Member (Jan 2025 – Present)
Designed and integrated embedded systems to monitor temperature, speed, strain, and stress. Programmed Arduino-based systems and developed protective strategies to ensure durability in a harsh concrete environment.

UBC Submarine Design Team (SUBC) – Hull and Frame Team Member
January 2026 - Present
I led the design and optimization of custom parametric structural connectors to minimize hull deformation and enhance load transfer in our human-powered submarine. Using SolidWorks, I modeled the connectors precisely to align with the hull and internal frame, ensuring a perfect fit and alignment. I collaborated closely with other sub-teams to ensure these connectors met structural, manufacturing, and assembly requirements. For the composite hull, I contributed to the manufacturing process, creating multi-layer fiberglass layups with carefully controlled fiber orientations to balance stiffness and strength.

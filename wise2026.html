WiSE 2026 Mentorship Night

Large-scale mentorship and networking event supporting women and underrepresented groups in STEM

March 25, 2026 Cynthia Hsiao

WiSE (Women in Science and Engineering) is a student-led initiative dedicated to promoting equity, representation, and professional development opportunities for women and underrepresented groups in STEM. The organization fosters mentorship, builds community, and creates accessible networking spaces that empower students to thrive in technical fields.

For WiSE 2026, an event attended by over 100 participants, I served as Co-Vice President, leading the planning and execution of one of our largest annual mentorship and networking events.

In this role, I managed and coordinated a team across multiple subcommittees, including Outreach, Event Coordination, and Supplies, ensuring alignment across all aspects of the event. I led regular meetings, delegated tasks, and worked closely with team members to track progress, troubleshoot challenges, and maintain timelines throughout the planning process.

I also played a key role in securing over $13,000 in sponsorship funding, building partnerships with organizations such as Klohn Crippen Berger (KCB), Hatch, Schneider Electric, ISL Engineering, Stantec, Thurber Engineering, Aspect Structural Engineers, YVR, COWI, and CIMA+. These contributions were critical in enhancing the scale and quality of the event.

Additionally, I supported event logistics and execution, including coordinating presentation materials, overseeing event-day operations, and ensuring a smooth and welcoming experience for attendees, mentors, and sponsors.

Through this experience, I developed strong skills in team leadership, stakeholder communication, sponsorship development, and large-scale event coordination, while contributing to an initiative focused on empowering the next generation of engineers.
